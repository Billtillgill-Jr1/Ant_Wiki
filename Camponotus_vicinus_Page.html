Species overview:

Camponotus vicinus or the variable carpenter ant is a semi-large species of black and red carpenter ant. They have majors and often nest in rotting wood leaving sawdust piles outside the nest. Nests can have thousands of workers and be very active.

Good to know:

Here is some good to know info about the species!

Scientific name and common name:

Camponotus vicinus-Variable Carpenter Ant

Native or invasive to Utah:

Camponotus vicinus is native

Are they Polygynous:

Camponotus vicinus is not

Do the larva spin cocoons or stay naked:

Camponotus vicinus larva spin cocoons

Are they claustral or semi-claustral:

Camponotus vicinus is a claustral species
