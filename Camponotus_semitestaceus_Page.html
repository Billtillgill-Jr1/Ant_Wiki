Species overview:

Camponotus semitestaceus or the brick carpenter ant is a large species of carpenter ant native to the western United States. Queens can be a bright yellow or a black and red. Camponotus semitestaceus has majors and nests can have thousands of workers. Colonies nest in soil and under stones.

Good to know:

Here is some good to know info about the species!

Scientific name and common name:

Camponotus semitestaceus-Brick Carpenter Ant

Native or invasive to Utah:

Camponotus semitestaceus is native

Are they Polygynous:

Camponotus semitestaceus is not

Do the larva spin cocoons or stay naked:

Camponotus semitestaceus larva spin cocoons

Are they claustral or semi-claustral:

Camponotus semitestaceus is a claustral species
